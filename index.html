Introduction

Our Journey

Generate music at the click of button

  • December 2020

    Our Humble Beginnings

    This project had started as a major school assignment where we could choose whatever interests we had to make a learning goal for my project. That learning goal was to generate music completely by the computer on its own using Artificial Intelligence

  • June 2021

    The Research Process

    This project idea was finalized and research had shortly began, a logical research plan was made that helped me identify the methods of my research and the types of sources I would use to get the most accurate and reliable information.

  • Mid-August 2021

    Product Process

    At this point of time, most of the research was done and it was time to fire up the environment and start coding, the process was slow but organized and accurate. After the backend for the product was eventually done, it was time to think about how I would advertise and start the frontend for my product!

  • November 2021

    Front-end

    There was another research session where I found out how I would advertise the product and create the front-end. I decided to make a website to advertise my product and planned to make it aesthetically appealing to clients, the website was soon over in the month of November and the project came to an end!
